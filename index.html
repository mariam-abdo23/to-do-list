How to maintain organization at work

Strategies for organizing the office

To organize your office and increase productivity, start by removing clutter and keeping only the necessary things. Use drawers or folders to store papers and documents. Keep your desktop as free as possible, keeping only the basics at hand. Take advantage of vertical space by using shelves to store unnecessary things. Manage the wires well with ties or clamps to keep them tidy. Finally, make time daily to clean your desk to ensure it stays tidy.
